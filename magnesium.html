Magnesium

Atomic Number: 12 Atomic Mass: 24.31

Magnesium is a lightweight element, that ignites easily in air.

It is also essentia; in both plant and animal life, without it photosythesis could not take place.

It is also used in all medicines. Magnesium Sulphate is used to mordant dyes.

Magnesium Hydroxide is used in plastics and also as a fire retardant.

Uses: Car seats, luggage, cameras, powertools, fire flares, fireworks and sparklers.
