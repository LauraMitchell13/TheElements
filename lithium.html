Lithium

Atomic Number: 3 Atomic Mass: 7

Lithium is a soft, silver material, discovered from a mineral.

It has the lowest density of all metals.

Uses: batteries, phones, laptops, pacemakers, toys clocks. Also used along with Alluminium and Magnesium to make car alloys, bicyle frames and trains.
